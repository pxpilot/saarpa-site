## Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Daily Moment of Zen application and the saarpa.com website. The app displays publicly available Instagram posts from Saar Paamoni's personal account. It does not collect, store, or process personal data from visitors.

## Data Collection

This site and application do not collect any personal information from visitors. There are no sign-up forms, user accounts, cookies, or analytics trackers on the /zen gallery page.

## Instagram Content

The gallery displays media from a personal Instagram account via the Instagram Graph API. This content consists of publicly shared posts. Instagram's own data practices are governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.

## Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on Cloudflare. Cloudflare may collect standard server logs (IP addresses, browser type) as part of normal web hosting operations. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details.

## Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please use the contact form at saarpa.com/contact.
